\# Partner Operations Engine



\## Purpose



The Partner Operations Engine models delegated administration and partner ecosystem workflows.



This engine is designed around public Microsoft partner administration concepts and is not tied to any specific employer or proprietary process.



\## Capabilities



\- GDAP relationship validation

\- GDAP role auditing

\- GDAP expiration monitoring

\- Partner Center licensing checks

\- Customer inventory

\- Cross-tenant reporting

\- Delegated access review

\- Stale engineer access detection



\## GDAP Health Checks



A GDAP health check should answer:



\- Which relationships are active?

\- Which relationships are expiring soon?

\- Which roles are assigned?

\- Which engineers have delegated access?

\- Are high-privilege roles assigned appropriately?

\- Are assignments group-based or direct?

\- Are stale users still present?

\- Are access paths documented?



\## Evidence Outputs



\- Customer tenant identifier

\- Relationship status

\- Expiration date

\- Assigned roles

\- Assigned groups

\- Assigned users

\- Risk level

\- Recommended action
